Spin

A powerful development environment manager that helps you run and manage multiple processes and services in your development workflow.

Get Started

Why Spin?

Zero to Productive

Get new developers up and running instantly with just two commands: spin fetch and spin up. No complex setup required, whether it's a Rails backend, React frontend, or both.

Team Consistency

Ensure every developer runs the same configuration, services, and processes. Eliminate "works on my machine" issues with standardized development environments.

Simple Yet Powerful

Leverages proven tools like Docker and Tmux behind the scenes, but keeps the developer experience clean and simple. No need to manage complex Docker configurations or process management.

Multi-Project Ready

Seamlessly manage multiple projects and their dependencies. Perfect for microservice architectures or full-stack development with separate frontend and backend repositories.
